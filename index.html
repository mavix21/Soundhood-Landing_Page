Features

Spotify Integration

Your user profile will be automatically generated and filled with your favorite artists, top tracks, and beloved albums. Experience a personalized journey from the moment you join.

Music-Based Social Network

Connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for music. Sounhood brings people together based on their musical tastes, creating a unique social networking experience centered around the music that moves you.

Personalized Recommendations

Sounhood intelligently suggests people who have the greatest musical affinity with you. Discover new connections through tailored recommendations, and choose to follow them or send messages to initiate conversations.

About Us

Miguel and Marcelo were two software engineering students at Holberton College, both share a deep passion for both technology and music, and often found themselves engaged in conversations about their favorite bands. During their studies at Holberton the opportunity to create a portfolio project arose, they immediately decided to combine their love of technology and music to create an application that would revolutionize the way people meet and connect.

Social Login

Log in with your Spotify account, just make two clicks without filling out boring forms and you are ready to meet new people!

Find Soundmates

Soundmates are people whose musical tastes are very similar to yours, our algorithm is in charge of putting them in front of you so you meet people who have the same interests as you.

Meet Concert Buddies

Say goodbye to those days when you went to any concert alone or didn't know who to go with, at Soundhood your soundmates will even invite you to events you didn't know about.
